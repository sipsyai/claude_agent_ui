# Autom Mate: Zero-Touch Service Delivery Platform

**Source:** https://autommate.com

---

## Overview

Autom Mate is an automation and integration platform that unifies AI, integration, and automation capabilities. The company describes their offering as enabling "Zero-Touch Service Delivery for Your Connected IT & AI Universe."

## Core Products

### Community Edition
Free integration and automation solution designed for individuals and small teams.

### Enterprise Edition
Combines Hyperflows with AI agents to deliver "Powerful, secure and scalable" service delivery at enterprise scale.

### MSP Edition
Multi-tenant solution launched in October 2025, adding advanced user access controls and multitenancy features to enterprise capabilities.

### AI Agent Composer
Tool enabling users to "Compose & orchestrate AI Agents across your entire IT stack" without requiring developer expertise.

## Key Solutions

The platform addresses three primary domains:

- **Service Management Orchestration** - Integrates ITSM platforms like ServiceNow, Jira, and Atlassian with communication and monitoring tools
- **Customer Experience Orchestration** - Enables automated service delivery through WhatsApp, Teams, and Slack
- **Business Technology Orchestration** - Connects IT operations, system management, DevOps, and monitoring systems

## Notable Features

The platform includes agentic AI capabilities for helpdesk automation, ITSM integration across DevOps ecosystems, real-time event remediation from monitoring platforms, and CMDB reconciliation across business systems.

## Certifications & Trust Indicators

Autom Mate achieved ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certification in August 2025, demonstrating commitment to information security standards.
